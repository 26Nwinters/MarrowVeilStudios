01 — Mission

We build horror that lives in the mind long after the screen goes dark.

MarrowVeil Studios exists to create narrative-driven psychological horror experiences with cinematic ambition. We believe the most resonant stories don't just scare — they expose something true about what it means to be human.

Our work explores memory, consequence, identity, and time distortion. Inspired by the narrative depth of games like Detroit: Become Human and the atmospheric dread of psychological horror, we craft worlds where every detail is intentional and every choice carries weight.

02 — The Founder
NITHANIEL
AURORA
WINTERS
N.A.W.
Founder · Director · Writer

Nithaniel Aurora Winters is an independent game developer and the founder of MarrowVeil Studios, a creative studio focused on narrative-driven horror experiences. His work centers on blending psychological storytelling with interactive systems, creating worlds that reward exploration, tension, and player-driven discovery.

With a strong focus on atmosphere and worldbuilding, Nithaniel is currently developing the Hourglass series — an interconnected set of games exploring memory, identity, and hidden truths beneath abandoned environments. Through MarrowVeil Studios, he aims to build immersive experiences that leave a lasting impact while continuing to grow a distinct and evolving creative voice.
