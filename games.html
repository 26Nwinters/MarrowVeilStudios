HOURGLASS: MISSING TIME
Narrative Horror · PC
Psychological Horror Narrative Mystery PC

Hourglass: Missing Time is a narrative-driven psychological horror game developed by MarrowVeil Studios. Players follow Boa Vandyke, a 19-year-old searching for her missing sister after receiving a cryptic message that leads her into a network of abandoned Meka Corporation locations. What begins as a personal investigation quickly spirals into something far more unsettling.

Blending stealth, exploration, and environmental puzzle-solving, players must navigate hostile animatronic units, uncover hidden evidence, and piece together the truth behind a long-buried corporate cover-up. As Boa pushes deeper, reality begins to fracture — and the closer she gets to answers, the more she risks becoming part of what she was trying to find.

02
MEK-AI ARCHIVES
Thriller Horror · Browser
ARG Interactive Fiction Puzzle Browser

Mek-Ai: Archives is a thriller-horror investigation experience developed by MarrowVeil Studios. Players access a compromised Meka Corporation laptop, guided by Ava Keep as they navigate a fractured archive system filled with corrupted files, missing employee records, and hidden communications. Through a fully interactive interface, players restore data, solve system-based puzzles, and unlock deeper clearance levels as the truth behind the network begins to surface.

As the investigation progresses, a corporate Fixer begins tracking the breach in real time, turning the experience into a balance between discovery and survival. Using surveillance cameras and environmental awareness, players must continue digging through the system while avoiding detection — because the deeper they go, the closer the Fixer gets.
