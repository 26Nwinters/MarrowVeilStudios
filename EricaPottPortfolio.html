About

Art rooted in
living things

Erica Potts is a multidisciplinary creative artist specializing in illustration, character design, and voice performance. Her work draws deeply from the natural world — mossy forest floors, twisted roots, the quiet wonder of something half-seen at the edge of the treeline.

With a background spanning nature illustration, fantasy character design, and horror-inflected graphic work, Erica brings an organic intuition to every project. She is a core member of MarrowVeil Studios, contributing visual identity and voice work to the HourGlass universe.
